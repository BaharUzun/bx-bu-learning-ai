The rear sensors are only active when the transmission is in reverse (R). As your vehicle moves closer to the obstacle, the rate of the audible warning increases. When the obstacle is less than 30  cm away, the warning sounds continuously. If the system detects a stationary or receding object farther than 30  cm from the corners of the bumper, the tone sounds for only three seconds. Once the system detects an object approaching, the warning sounds again.

Coverage area of up to 1.8  m from the rear bumper. There may be decreased coverage area at the outer corners of the bumper.

The system provides audio warnings only when your vehicle is moving or when your vehicle is stationary and the detected obstacle is less than 30  cm away from the bumper.
